Classic American Cheeseburger

Hometown classic that everyone enjoys. I make this recipe all year round for my hungry kids!

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 Tbsp MSG
  • (optional) 10 Slices of your favorite cheese
  • (optional) 10 Strips of bacon

How to Make:

  1. Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook patties for about 3â€“4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. Add cheese on top during the last minute of cooking if desired.
  5. Toast burger buns lightly.
  6. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and condiments.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade burger!
