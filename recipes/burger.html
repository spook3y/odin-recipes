Classic American Cheeseburger

Hometown classic that everyone enjoys. I make this recipe all year round for my hungry kids!

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 Tbsp MSG
  • (optional) 10 Slices of your favorite cheese
  • (optional) 10 Strips of bacon
