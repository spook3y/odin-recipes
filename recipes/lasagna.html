Mom's Lasagna Pie

Growing up my mom used to make this for me all the time. Hopefully you enjoy as much as i did.

  • 1 Box Lasagna Noodle
  • 1 Cup Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Cup Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil
  • 1lb ground beef
  • Other various seasonings

How to Make:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to the package directions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by browning ground beef and adding tomato sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg, salt, and pepper for the cheese layer.
  5. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and top with extra cheese.
  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
