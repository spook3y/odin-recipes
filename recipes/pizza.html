Dad's Italian Pie

My dad used to make me help hand prepare these pies as a kid. Growing up I didnt appreciate the extra effort. Now I am happy to impress all my friends when I am hosting for dinner.

  • 4 Cups flour
  • 3 Packets Instant Yeast
  • Prepared Sauce if you're lazy like me
  • 2 Cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil
  • Fresh Cherry Tomato
  • Love

How to Make:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the dough by rolling it out into a circle on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  7. Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
